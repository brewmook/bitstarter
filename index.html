Syflo

Sort your flippin' life out!

Easily distracted?

You're a naturally curious person, you like to learn. The problem is you want to know everything. Last week you learned how to read a basic Chinese menu. Great, but will you remember any of it in two weeks time? And what about that app you started writing? Ah yes, you forgot about it when you (re)discovered Coursera.

Take a step back and look at everything you've started, but not yet finished. Decide what's important, what is most valuable to you in the long run. Focus your attention on something you think you can actually complete, and finish it off.

Learn to focus

Syflo can help you analyse the extent of the problem. It will pester you regularly and ask you what you're doing, giving you a short list to choose from. It only takes a few seconds, and you can get back to happily procrastinating with whatever side-track you were going down. Then you can ask for graphs of everything you've been up to, and see where all your valuable time has gone. You may be surprised at the amount of time you spend on stuff that can wait until later. Analyse your data to learn when distractions aren't paying off and nip them in the bud.
